We have been studying [Jacob 5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/jacob/5?lang=eng) in the Book of Mormon this week which is The Allegory of the Olive Trees.

A couple months ago, I got this edition of [The Millennial Instructor](https://www.millennialstandardpress.com/) that has really been helping us to better understand the scattering and gathering of Israel. It also has a lot of fun ideas, activities, and recipes that have to do with olives and olive trees so we decided to do a Jacob 5 themed Fika today.

![](/uploads/2020/03/IMG_8061.jpg)

The kids made olive trees (with good and bad fruit) from legos; we had lemon olive oil cookies, and carrot olives (a family "recipe" and invention of my Grandma Hall); and we made an olive oil lamp from an orange (the pith makes a natural wick).
