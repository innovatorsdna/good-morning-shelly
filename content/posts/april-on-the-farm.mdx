![](/uploads/2021/06/IMG_5532.jpeg) Our fruit trees arrived at the end of the month and we spent an evening planting all 17 trees. We've got 3 peach (Redhaven, Flamin'Fury and Reliance), 2 Nectarine (Hardired and Fantasia ), 3 cherry (BlackGold, Bing, and Stella), 2 Plum (Methley and Santa Rosa), 3 Apple (Winecrisp, Gala, and Golden Delicious), 2 European Pear (Moonglow and Sunrise), 2 Asian Pear (Yoinashi and Korean Giant) ... so with the fruit cocktail tree that we already planted in March, that brings us up to 18 fruit trees. ... we might be crazy. But you know what they say "The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now."
