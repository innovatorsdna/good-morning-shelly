One of the countries that we have been learning a little bit about this month is Canada. Jershon served his [mission](https://www.comeuntochrist.org/belong/meet-missionaries) in Alberta, Canada 15+ years ago so he put together a little Canada themed Fika for us. We ate Pirogies (dipped in sour cream) and Poutine (french fries with gravy and cheese curds). Jershon also told us about some of the areas that he served in and he taught us how to say some Canadian words.
