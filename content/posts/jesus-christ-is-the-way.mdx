- "And I will send upon him the Comforter, **which shall teach him the truth and the way whither he shall go**;" ([Doctrine & Covenants 79:2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/79.2?lang=eng\&clang=eng#p1))

Christ is the _way_. _**He**_ is the space that is opened up for us - both that we might escape bondage, and that we may enter into His rest. He was literally opened up in the Garden of Gethsemane. He was filled with compassion and mercy to overflowing and blood poured out through the openings in His skin (pores) all over His entire body. When the blood of the lamb was on the door frames and when The Lamb was on the cross, He was the [opening of the doorway](/passover-2020/). And joyously, on Easter Sunday, He showed final victory when the tomb was opened and He rose again. He isn't only _the way_ that we should do things, as in _the example_ (although He definitely is that). He is _literally_ the way - the _opening_ for both our escape and our entrance. There are plenty of good examples to follow in the world (although not perfect), but no one else can provide the _opening_, the _space_, the _gap_. ![](/uploads/2020/04/The_Gate_Shopify_full_1024x1024@2x.jpg) [The Gate by Jenedy Page](https://store.jenedypaige.com/products/the-gate)

> "Then said Jesus unto them again, Verily, verily, I say unto you, **I am the door of the sheep**. All that ever came before me are thieves and robbers: but the sheep did not hear them. **I am the door**: by me if any man enter in, he shall be saved, and shall go in and out, and find pasture." ([John 10:7-9](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/10.7,9?lang=eng\&clang=eng#p6))

Now is the time for us to prepare the way, to make an open space, in our hearts, in our homes, in the world - so that Jesus Christ may come again. "But behold, the Spirit hath said this much unto me, saying: Cry unto this people, saying—**Repent ye, and prepare the way of the Lord**, and walk in his paths, which are straight; for behold, the kingdom of heaven is at hand, and the Son of God cometh upon the face of the earth." ([Alma 7:9](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/7.9?lang=eng\&clang=eng#p8)) "For behold, the Lord God hath sent forth the angel crying through the midst of heaven, saying: **Prepare ye the way of the Lord**, and make his paths straight, for the hour of his coming is nigh-" ([Doctrine & Covenants 133:17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/133.17?lang=eng\&clang=eng#p16))

And a couple of scripture verses to wrap all of this up...

"Angels speak by the power of the Holy Ghost; wherefore, they speak the words of Christ. Wherefore, I said unto you, feast upon the words of Christ; for behold, the words of Christ will tell you all things what ye should do. Wherefore, now after I have spoken these words, if ye cannot understand them it will be because ye ask not, neither do ye knock; wherefore, ye are not brought into the light, but must perish in the dark. For behold, again I say unto you that if ye will enter in by the way, and receive the Holy Ghost, it will show unto you all things what ye should do." ([2 Nephi 32:3-5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/32.4?lang=eng\&clang=eng#p3))

“Let him that is ignorant learn wisdom by humbling himself and calling upon the Lord his God, that his eyes may be opened that he may see, and his ears opened that he may hear; For my Spirit is sent forth into the world to enlighten the humble and contrite, and to the condemnation of the ungodly.” ([Doctrine & Covenants 136:32-33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/136.32?lang=eng\&clang=eng#p31))

Opening our ears, eyes, hearts, and minds is the key to receiving personal revelation. The Spirit of God enters our hearts and minds and fills us with what we need. Jesus Christ is the way - the only way - to gain peace in this life, and eternal salvation in the life to come.
