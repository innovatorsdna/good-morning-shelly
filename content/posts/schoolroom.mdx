Today is our first official day of homeschooling (after doing bits and pieces of it for the past year) and I wanted to share a glimpse into our schoolroom.I started creating this room last September (even though, at the time, I didn’t think I would get to actually homeschool full time) and have been adding to it over the past several months. This is one of my favorite rooms in our house. I love spending time in this room. And even though learning will (and already has) happen all over our house (and outside our home), this room is a of home base of sorts.

![](/uploads/2020/02/IMG_1359.jpg) ![](/uploads/2020/02/IMG_1364.jpg)

![](/uploads/2019/09/IMG_1469.jpg) The theme that I felt impressed to choose for our family/school this year is "[I have learned for myself](/i-have-learned-for-myself/)." The lilacs are explained on [this](/swedish-unit-visiting-swen-and-thildas-graves/) post. We have already made so many great memories in this room and I am excited to make even more.
