Tonight we had Lesson 6 in our Family Proclamation Unit: We have a Divine Destiny. "_...and gain earthly experience to progress toward perfection and ultimately realize his or her divine destiny as heirs of eternal life._" We popped some popcorn and talked about how we are like the popcorn kernal and God is like the popped pop corn. We have a divine nature and potential within us in seed form and as we learn and grow throughout our lives (and become perfected in the next life), we eventually become like God.
