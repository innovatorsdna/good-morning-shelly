(This is post #3 about our garden. See #1 [here](/gardening-month-one/) and #2 [here](/gardening-month-two/).) When I was looking up information and ideas for our indoor garden, I ran across this book... Year-Round Salad Gardening by Peter Burke. I checked it out from the library and read the whole book in a few days (which is pretty fast for me).

![](/uploads/2020/03/IMG_7744-1.jpg)

Then I got the supplies and we tried it out! Now we've grown and harvested these sprouts 4 times (and could have grown them many more times than that in the past 2 months). We have grown Snow Pea, Buckwheat, and Sunflower sprouts. The process is super easy and the whole thing, from start (soaking the seeds) to finish (harvesting) only takes about 10 days.
