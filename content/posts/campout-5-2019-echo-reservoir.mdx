Campout number 5 for 2019: Echo Reservoir. Forgot the swimming suits so the kids just waded around in their clothes. Haha. We made some awesome (healthy? no) fried chicken in the Dutch oven. Slack lined over the water, went fishing (no luck), threw a lot of rocks in the water, found a cool Robin’s egg shell, and the boys practiced their whittling skills. Another successful campout!
