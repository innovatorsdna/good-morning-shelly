-pregnancy is a vulnerable time, especially as we prepare for birth _(especially&#x20;_&#x6E;atural birth). We need to protect our delicate minds and stay away from negative opinions, stories, thoughts, etc. This should be done throughout our regular, non pregnant lives as well. http\://goodmorningshelly.com/higher-thoughts-inspiring-my-best-imagination/ -strong reliance on God and turning to Him often because there are so many moments of anxiety, worry, fear, desire for peace and reassurance. There is so much surrendering that has to happen as we know that there is so much outside of our control. A lot of faith and hope is required.
