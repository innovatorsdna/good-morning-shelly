Yesterday we finished our Family Proclamation Unit. Since we only did one or two lessons each week it took us about 3 months to complete the unit. I used awesome lesson plans from a blog that I found (you can find the lesson plans [here](https://www.pinterest.com/shellylm/unit-studies/family-proclamation-unit/)). I highly recommend those lesson plans. :) It has been really great to take some intentional time to teach my kids about the importance of families and how we can strengthen our family to fulfill God’s plan.

![](/uploads/2019/01/IMG_6764.jpg)

Our next unit (which we are starting today) is our Swedish Unit. I have been preparing for this unit over the past few weeks and I am suuuuppper excited about this one because I have Swedish heritage (my great grandparents immigrated from Sweden). I’ll probably be sharing some of our lessons on here.
