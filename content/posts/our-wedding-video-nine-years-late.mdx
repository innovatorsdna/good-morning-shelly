We spent Thanksgiving at my parents' house in Missouri. One of my biggest regrets from our wedding is not getting any video clips. I knew that we would be coming to Nauvoo as part of this trip so I came up with the idea to bring my wedding dress and have my siblings take some video clips that I could put together. So that's what we did. We got to Nauvoo at about 4:00 pm and I knew that we would have to be quick so we could get some photos/video before the sun went down. When we got to Nauvoo we went straight to the temple. I had to change into my wedding dress in the van. Haha. It was fun to pretend like it was our wedding day again and to wear my dress. Jershon was a good sport and helped my dreams of a wedding video come true. :)
