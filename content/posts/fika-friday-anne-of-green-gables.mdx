Last January, we read Anne of Green Gables and then watched the 1985 version of the movie. We all loved both the book and the movie, but Roxanne especially. She has been quite enamored with Anne of Green Gables for the past year. So for Christmas, Santa brought her a dress with puffed sleeves and a straw hat. She has been wearing this outfit nearly everyday since then... only taking a break from the dress if it's being washed.

![](/uploads/2021/01/IMG_6462.jpg)

![](/uploads/2021/01/IMG_6459.jpg)

This week for Fika, we decided to have an Anne of Green Gables theme. Roxanne has been super excited about this and made sure that we included several foods from the book. We made [Raspberry Cordial](https://www.mirlandraskitchen.com/anne-of-green-gables-raspberry-cordial/) (not to be confused with Currant Wine), [Anne's Vanilla Layer Cake](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NkUqFn7iHhk\&feature=share) (no liniment), [Marilla's Plum Pudding](https://www.food.com/recipe/marillas-plum-pudding-anne-of-green-gables-362240) with [Carmel Sauce](https://www.anneofgreengables.com/recipes/recipes-from-avonlea-caramel-pudding-sauce) (luckily no drowned mouse this time), homemade bread, [homemade jam](https://www.anneofgreengables.com/recipes/recipes-from-avonlea-homemade-jam) and carrots (no slates were smashed on anyone's head).
