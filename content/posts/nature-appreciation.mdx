August 24th: For the past few weeks, a hummingbird keeps randomly coming to our little garden on our deck. I had never been able to capture it on photo or video because it would only hang around for a few seconds at a time. But today, when we were sitting at the table eating lunch, we saw the hummingbird again! I quickly grabbed my camera from the counter and started recording video footage. This time it stayed, sipping nectar from Evelyn's flowers, for more than 30 seconds! It was fun to watch. It flew away so I turned off my camera, which I wish I hadn't because a few seconds later it flew over to the sliding door and hovered in the air about 18 inches from my face (with the glass in between us) for a few seconds. It was looking right at me! So awesome!
