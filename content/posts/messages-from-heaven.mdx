Yesterday morning, the kids and I watched an old Shirley Temple movie from the 1940s called The Blue Bird. I hadn't ever seen it before and didn't know what it was about but it had been highly recommended so we decided to watch it and we really enjoyed it - I especially liked the messages and life lessons that it contains. I wanted to share my 2 favorite parts and why... First, this scene (time stamp 25:27 - 34:00 minutes) where the little girl (Mytyl) and her brother (Tytyl) see their deceased grandparents.Here are the lines that particularly stood out to me in that scene...

> Granny: "Somebody must be thinking of us. I'm beginning to feel quite strong."
>

This scene reminded me of a quote that I really like and that I've mentioned on a few of my other posts in the past...

> “There are three deaths. The first is when the body ceases to function. The second is when the body is consigned to the grave. The third is that moment, sometime in the future, when your name is spoken for the last time.” (David Eagleman)

I believe that my ancestors really are still alive... just in a different way for the time being. I believe that their bodies are in the grave but their spirits are very much alive, in the [spirit world](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/manual/gospel-principles/chapter-41-the-postmortal-spirit-world?lang=eng). They are awaiting resurrection, when their bodies and spirits will be reunited once more, never to be separated again. I believe that the spirit world is not so far away as we might think. In fact, I believe that it is all around us... we just can't see through the veil that separates us from them. ...I do believe that our ancestors can see through the veil to us though. I believe they are more a part of our daily lives than we may realize.

> "Latter-day prophets have said that the spirits of those who have died are not far from us. President Ezra Taft Benson said: “Sometimes the veil between this life and the life beyond becomes very thin. Our loved ones who have passed on are not far from us” (in Conference Report, Apr. 1971, 18; or _Ensign,_ June 1971, 33). President Brigham Young taught that the postmortal spirit world is on the earth, around us (see _Teachings of Presidents of the Church: Brigham Young_ \[1997], 279)."

I want my ancestors to live on

After Roxanne, we were pretty sure that we were done having babies... but we were open to revelation about having another one (or more?), if that was God's will for our family. As the years went by, and I hadn't received any super strong impressions, I got used to the idea of being done with that chapter of life (although I was sad about it at times). But then in January, I started feeling major impressions that perhaps we weren't done after all. It took a few months for me to be brave enough to really go all in and step out in complete faith. ...and then we were riskier for a couple of months... and then we finally decided to fully step out in faith and keep the doors wide open and see what happened. I got pregnant on the second month of doing that. Over the last 3.5 years, (almost all of these being in 2020), I have had 8 dreams about having another baby... at least that I recorded in my journal. I think I'll copy all of these over in my next letter to you. Evelyn has had 2 dreams, that she told me about at least. Porter made a comment back in 2018 that "Roxanne is a big sister to someone in heaven." And Evelyn and Roxanne have been making tons of comments to me over the past few months (without any prompting from me) about how I am having another baby. 3 days after I ovulated this cycle (that I ended up getting pregnant on) the girls came into my room and told me that I was going to have another baby. Then Evelyn said to Roxanne, very matter of factly, "go downstairs and tell dadd that mommy just found out that she is going to have another baby!" haha. In January, Evelyn had a dream that we had another baby and it was a girl. She told me that morning. Then she told Jershon that night... he said "mom did have a baby girl... it's this one..." and then he hugged Roxanne. But then Evelyn said "No. In my dream you had 5 kids."
