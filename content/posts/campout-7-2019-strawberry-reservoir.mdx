We went camping again last night. This time we went to Strawberry Reservoir. Lots of bugs at this campout. More than any of our other campouts combined. But all in all, it was still a good campout. Our campsite wasn't the best logistically... it wasn't very flat and the lack of shade and bugs made it less pleasant. But looks wise, it was really beautiful. We were up in the aspen trees and we had tons of trees around us. Our campsite was relatively secluded ... even though it was next to the road. We didn't have other campsites right next to us like usual.
