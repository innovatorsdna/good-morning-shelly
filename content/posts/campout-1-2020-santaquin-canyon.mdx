We went camping for the first time in 2020! After trying to camp in Payson Canyon but finding the gates still closed, we ended up in Santaquin Canyon (right near the entrance) at a campsite that ended up not being a real campsite (but the nice guy said we could stay anyway). It wasn't too cold and it rained for most of the night (so much for that 20% chance of rain!) but we stayed nice and dry in our tent. All in all, it was a good first campout of the season! I loved seeing all the bright green colors of spring.
