Yesterday, on a whim, I decided to rearrange and reorganize in the schoolroom. If you're curious about how it used to be set up, you can see that [here](/schoolroom/). A lot is still the same, but I made some changes too. We've acquired quite a few books this year for the Well-Educated Heart [monthly rotation](https://www.welleducatedheart.com/rotation.html) and I wanted to figure out how to have all of our books in the same place instead of spread out all over the house. So I pulled a couple of bookshelves that we already had from other areas of the house (and then had to rearrange that too) and put them in the schoolroom instead -one in the main part of the room, and one (along with the two smaller ones that were already in there) in the closet. I also pulled the piano out of the closet and miraculously, everything fit!
