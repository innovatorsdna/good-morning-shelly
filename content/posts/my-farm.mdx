Last week, Evelyn and Roxanne were working on writing and illustrating more of their own books (using [Bare Books](https://www.barebooks.com/)). ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5600-667x500.jpeg) ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5597-667x500.jpeg)

![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5582-667x500.jpeg)

As they were showing them to me, I was reminded of the books that I wrote when I was about their ages. I had the same teacher, Mrs. Morrison for 1st and 2nd grade. During these years, I was really into writing and illustrating my own stories. Mrs. Morrison always took the time to make me feel like the simple stories I wrote were important and special and she would get them bound in some way to make me feel like an official author. I decided to pull out my box of journals and other mementos from my childhood and I found my little books to show to my girls. ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5584-375x500.jpeg) The white books at the top of this photo are Evelyn and Roxanne's books and all the others are books that I wrote when I was in 1st and 2nd grade. It was fun to read through some of them and I especially liked finding the farm book. ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5589.jpeg) ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5590.jpeg) I am moving on a farm. ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5591.jpeg) The farm has a tree house. ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5592-667x500.jpeg) It has a good house. ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5594-667x500.jpeg) I like the farm. ![](/uploads/2021/07/IMG_5595-667x500.jpeg) It is awesome. I wrote this book in February 1995 when I was in 1st grade. It was during the time when my parents were looking for a farm to buy and we had gone to see one in person. I thought we were going to buy it. …but we didn’t end up getting a house with some land until 10 years later and I only lived there for a couple years before heading off to college. … but now here I am, 25 years after writing that little book, and I finally have my farm. So I decided to write an updated version of my book...
