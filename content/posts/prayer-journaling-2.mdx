Heaven Journaling Sandwich Asking a question, writing it down, opening the scriptures, listening for the answer, writing it down ... listening for other revelation as well that maybe wasn't even what you specifically asked -pouring out and allowing God to hold space -making a personal prayer list where you write down what you feel prompted to pray about and then taking that list with you to your "meeting with God".
