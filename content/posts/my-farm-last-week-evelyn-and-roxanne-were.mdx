**My Farm**

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

Last week, Evelyn and Roxanne were working on writing and illustrating more of their own books (using [Bare Books](https://www.barebooks.com/)).

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

As they were showing them to me, I was reminded of the books that I wrote when I was about their ages.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

I had the same teacher, Mrs. Morrison for 1st and 2nd grade. During these years, I was really into writing and illustrating my own stories. Mrs. Morrison always took the time to make me feel like the simple stories I wrote were important and special and she would get them bound in some way to make me feel like an official author.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

I decided to pull out my box of journals and other mementos from my childhood and I found my little books to show to my girls.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

I wrote this book in February 1995 when I was in 1st grade. It was during the time when my parents were looking for a farm to buy and we had gone to see one in person. I thought we were going to buy it. …but we didn’t end up getting a house with some land until 10 years later and I only lived there for a couple years before heading off to college.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

… but now here I am, 25 years after writing that little book, and I finally have my farm.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

So I decided to write an updated version of my book...
