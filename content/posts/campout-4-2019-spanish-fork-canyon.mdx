Campout 4 for 2019! We went up Spanish Fork Canyon and it was so pretty and green up there... except for all of the black burned trees from the huge wildfire last summer. We hiked up the mountain, played in the tall grass, picked wild flowers, and watched Jershon slack line across the mini river. It was so nice to get some fresh mountain air and relax in nature.
