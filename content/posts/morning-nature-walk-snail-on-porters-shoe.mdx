We have started going on a walk every morning before breakfast. I really want to bring more nature into our daily life and sometimes I feel so confined, living in a suburban area. So even though it doesn’t feel exactly the same as walking through the woods, walking around our neighborhood is at least better than just staying inside our house. It is so nice to be outside early in the morning when the air is still cool and everything is still quiet. Yesterday morning we got lucky and got to see a snail. The kids loved watching it. It even crawled up onto Porter’s shoe. :)
