Starting around the 1 hour and 23 minutes mark to about the 1 hour and 34 minute mark God sets things up unfairly and then watches what we do with what He has given us. Treat each other in such a way that we will right what looks like wrongs that God set up. God sets it up in an unequal, unfair way, but by His very nature as a just and perfectly equitable Father, He wants us to get to the point where we will be one. Life is set up in such a way that we have a chance to prove ourselves and to learn equality. Along the way, the rich will learn lessons and the poor will learn lessons. They will learn lessons as they become one with another. And in the process of becoming one, they will become like God.

> "I've sometimes used wind as an example of this because wind is a good example of inequality seeking equality. Nature abhors a vacuum and nature always seeks equilibrium. Nature is like the God of Nature who is just and has no respecter of persons. Total equality is what nature is after. So if there is an area of high pressure and an area of low pressure, then that's what causes the wind. It will seek it's own equilibrium. Think about water flowing. If there is water at a higher place, that has more potential energy. Water, all it ever does, is seek the easiest way to get down hill. And what's that doing? It's trying to seek equilibrium. Water is always on the level, or in pursuit of it. Well, then why not have all barometric pressure equal around the clock and around the world? Or why not just keep water always at the level? If nature wants that equilibrium, let it have it. Well, again, that's the power of setting it up with inequality, knowing that nature will inexorably seek equilibrium. Because guess what? Wind and water flow can do a lot of good. There is power that comes through that flow. With no wind, there's no wind turbines, there's no energy, there's not power being produced. Think about mills and water wheels and all the power that comes when that potential energy is given off in other dimensions. Even within the human body, since the body is always seeking equilibrium, I've seen in different medical procedures where they will send a certain, whether it's iodine or some kind of an isotope for medication...if we can high concentrate in this part of the body, the body will naturally circulate it through so that it gets to the areas of low concentration. Once again, there is power that comes through the flow. God can actually get more mileage out of His equality if He doesn't start us there. If He tells us "this is my intended destination... but this is where you're going to start...now find each other. Allow the wind to blow and the water to flow. Those who have, give to those who have not, and you will learn. Those who have not, don't just complain to Me, seek an understanding and have the humility to ask for assistance." ... The highs and the lows, the haves and the have nots, and what each party has to contribute and what each party needs to overcome. There are strengths and weaknesses on both sides of the equation, but again, that's part of the power of the flow and what God is trying to do." (Jared Halverson)
