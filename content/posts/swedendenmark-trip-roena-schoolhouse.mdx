(journal entry from August 26, 2019) After lunch, Erik had my mom and I ride with him (Jershon and my dad were in the rental car) and we drove over to Röena to the schoolhouse. THE [schoolhouse](/sweden-and-denmark-trip-some-backstory/) where Swen and Thilda and their kids lived in one side and the school (where Swen taught) was on the other side. This is where Dan was born (along with 6 more of their 10 children). It was so neat to stand outside and walk around this very place. I wish we could have gone inside but it is owned by someone else now and they use it as their summerhouse.
