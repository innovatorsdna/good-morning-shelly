One of my favorite parts about where I live is that I live so close to a temple. I feel very fortunate to live in a place that is surrounded by temples. My favorite time to go to the temple is early in the morning when it's still dark outside. There is something symbolic about going in when its dark and coming out when its light. The temple is a place where I find rest for my soul and respite from the storms of life. It is the place where I gain energy and power to continue on my journey home.
