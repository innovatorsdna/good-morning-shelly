A little while ago, I learned about a great idea from Mikael, called [Morning Menus](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0dGeCtx0weY) and we've been incorporating this idea for a few weeks now. So far it's going great! We had already been doing a song, prayer, and scripture study during our morning time, but I had been wanting to figure out how to incorporate more memory work as well and these menus make it easy to be consistent. It's also a great way to have daily reminders of what we are working on.

![](/uploads/2020/05/IMG_9959.jpg)

In our menus, we have, our [theme](/i-have-learned-for-myself/), the new [Restoration Proclamation](https://newsroom.churchofjesuschrist.org/multimedia/file/restoration-proclamation-2020-april.pdf) that we are memorizing (one line a week)...

![](/uploads/2020/05/IMG_9960.jpg)

the song that we are learning for the month, our goals for the [children and youth program](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/youth/childrenandyouth?lang=eng)...

![](/uploads/2020/05/IMG_9961.jpg)

a quote to memorize (this month's is about the [Word of Wisdom](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/manual/gospel-topics/word-of-wisdom?lang=eng) since we are learning about the human body this month), a poem that we are memorizing...

![](/uploads/2020/05/IMG_9962.jpg)

...and a painting that we are memorizing. Memorizing artwork may sounds strange, but we want to [fill our minds](/memorizing-good-and-beautiful-things/) and hearts with good images. To memorize the paintings, I just set the timer on my phone for 1 minute and we quietly look at the painting for 1 minute while we look for different details that we like or hadn't noticed yet. When the timer goes off, we go around the table and share what we found/like. I switch out the painting to a new one when we have the other one well memorized (takes a week or so).

![](/uploads/2020/05/IMG_9964.jpg)
