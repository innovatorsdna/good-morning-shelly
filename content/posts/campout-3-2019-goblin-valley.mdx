Campout # 3 of 2019! We usually camp about an hour or less away from home but this time we decided to go to Goblin Valley which is 3 hours from our house. We lucked out and were able to find an awesome campsite, complete with our own private slot canyon (or cave as the kids liked to call it). We also spent some time “hiking” around Goblin Valley and looking at all of the neat rock formations. The weather was awesome and we had a great time. :)
