I'm just your average [Latter-day Saint](https://www.lds.org/?lang=eng) girl who happens to be passionate about personal growth, family history, and teaching my children. I write about my life and morning moments - both literal and figurative.

The literal morning is meaningful to me because I do an early morning personal development routine everyday that has blessed my life for the past 6 years.

The figurative morning is meaningful to me because of the light that these moments bring to my life. Moments where I feel dark, confused, alone, afraid, etc. But then something crosses my path (sometimes this takes minutes, sometimes years,) and I start to see the light. I experience understanding, clarity, and joy.

Welcome to my little corner of the internet where I share my thoughts about what I am learning and how I am applying those tools to my own everyday life.

\*I don't claim to know everything and this blog does not represent the official doctrine of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. These are just my personal thoughts and interpretations. To learn more about official church doctrine, see [churchofjesuschrist.org](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/?lang=eng).
